About

Fire Force (Japanese: 炎炎ノ消防隊 Hepburn: enen no shouboutai,”fire brigade of flames”) is a Japanese shōnen manga series written and illustrated by Atsushi Ōkubo. It is published by Kodansha and has been serialized in the Weekly Shōnen Magazine since September 23, 2015, with the chapters collected into twenty tankōbon volumes as of October 2019
